# Soared Resume

AI-powered resume scoring for Claude Cowork. Drop your resume in and get instant feedback with actionable fixes.

## Install

Download this folder and add it to your Cowork plugins directory. That's it.

## What You Get

**Instant Resume Score** — Drop any resume (PDF, Word, or text) and get a 0-100 score with the top issues holding you back.

**Role-Specific Tips** — Get targeted advice for your field: engineering, product, marketing, design, and more.

## Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---------|--------------|
| `/resume-score` | Score your resume, see top 3-5 issues, get quick wins |
| `/resume-tips` | Get best practices for your specific role |

## How It Works

1. Drop your resume into the conversation (or paste the text)
2. Claude analyzes it against proven best practices
3. You get a score, top issues, and what to fix first
